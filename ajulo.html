About.

Raheem Tunji Olawuyi (commonly called Ajuloopin) is a Nigerian politician. He is a member of the Federal House of Representatives of Nigeria representing Ekiti, Irepodun, Isin, and Oke-Ero federal constituency of Kwara State. He is the House Committee Chairman on Emergency and Disaster Preparedness. In 2018, the Ekiti, Irepodun, Isin, and Oke-Ero Federal Constituency Bye-Election, he contested across the four Local Governments in Kwara, and he was declared in November as having been won by Raheem Olawuyi.
