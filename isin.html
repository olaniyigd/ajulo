Isin Local Government Constituency Project.

Reinstallation of Transformer.

Hon. Ajulo ordered the repair and installation of Transformers serving the people of Federal area community in Omu-Aran, Irepodun LG.

Construction of Road.

Hon. Ajulo will continue trying everything possible to touch every nooks and crannies of our constituency. In one of the promises he made, the construction of Omu-Aran township roads, starting from Olomu Palace has began.

Installation of Solar Power.

Hon. Ajulo made a promise to the people of Ekiti LG to better the constituency than the way he met it. He has taken a step forward with an installation of Solar Power in irepodun Local Government.

Provision of Water

As one of Hon. Ajulo's six agenda is to provide water and better sanitation, to this end, he has provided water simultaneously in Irepodun LG.

Reconstruction of Bridge.

Hon. Ajulo has began the reconstruction of abandone Oloogoro/GRA bridge in Omu-Aran, Irepodun LG.

Distribution of Chairs and Desks to Schools.

Hon. Ajulo distributed 32 sets of Chairs and Desks to Isapa Community School Okesa, in Ekiti Local Government.

Support with food.

Hon. Ajulo support the residents of Ekiti Local Government with food items as the rigour of the lockdown bites.
